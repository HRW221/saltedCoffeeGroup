About us

Welcome to Guardians of the Garden, the ultimate game for exploring the lush biodiversity thriving on your campus!

Delve into the enchanting world of flora as you embark on an exciting journey to discover, collect, and learn about the diverse plant species scattered across our campus. Collect varieties of exotic and native plants across campus and compete with friends to find the most plants for your garden. Join university and society led leaderboards to follow how friends and classmates are doing.

How to use Guardians of the Garden

Collect unique cards to earn points, with rarer finds offering greater rewards, and complete packs to unlock even more points. Let the adventure begin!

Collect

Take photos of plants around campus to identify them. Collect plants from our campus plant packs to add them to your garden.

Earn Points

Collect points each time you find one of our plants and gain extra points if you complete a pack or find the plant of the day!

Packs

Discover themed packs based on different places around campus.

Learn

Access information about each plant in your collection to deepen your knowledge of biodiversity and nature.

Compete

Compete with friends or other players on leaderboards to see who has the most extensive collection and highest score.

Share

Share your discoveries and achievements with others and collaborate to find and learn about nature at the University of Exeter.
